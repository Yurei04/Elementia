How To Play The Game


Objective: Defeat the computer by strategically selecting elemental combinations to counter its choices and reach the score limit first.

Game Mechanics: Choose your primary elemental vision to begin the clash against the computer's first choice. Select your secondary elemental vision to counter the computer's second choice. The chosen elemental visions will be combined to form a reaction, which will then counter the computer's elemental reaction. Keep track of scores; the first player to reach the score limit wins.

Elemental Choices and Counter Elements:
Pyro - Counter: Hydro Hydro - Counter: Electro Electro - Counter: Cryo Cryo - Counter: Pyro Anemo - Counter: Geo Geo - Counter: Dendro

Reactions and Counter Reactions:
Pyro + Hydro = Vaporize - Counter: Cryo Hydro + Electro = Electro-Charged - Counter: Pyro Electro + Cryo = Superconduct - Counter: Hydro Cryo + Pyro = Melt - Counter: Electro Anemo + Geo = Crystallize - Counter: Dendro Geo + Dendro = Burning - Counter: Anemo

Score Limit: The score limit can be customized based on player preference or set to a default value (e.g., first to reach 10 points).
